Keisha Watkin, often associating herself as Tara (middle name) is a New York native who discovered a love for the arts. As a young girl, Keisha had been fascinated by the digital work within the world and the way it visually has an impact on one. Unintentionally, Keisha had stumbled onto the same feelings as she grew older and stuck to the fascination. She often found herself staring at animation movies and other digital art works that left her wondering how a person was able to do their art. Getting her hands on different softwares, she had learned her skills as a first knowledge basis and started to develop an understanding.

However, the first knowledge basis wasn't enough for Keisha and she no longer wanted her art to be a hobby. Keisha wanted to share her work with the world, inspiring other people that enjoyed the same category of art. Knowing that her skills are still at its prime stage, Keisha goes to Queensborough Community College for Digital Art & Design to help better herself.

As of now, Keisha's projected graduation date is May 2016. While she adventures into the world and begins to discover herself in the world, she does small side projects to help her friends out along with doing her own for enjoyment in hopes of being able to do freelance work. Leading on in life, she hopes to travel for inspiration then find work within the music or animation industry with the skills that she will learn and create an image that can inspire those who are like her.
